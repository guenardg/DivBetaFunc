Matrices de données R sur les traits fonctionnels

• adespatial:Tiahura
280 fish species observer at 22 sites, 22 habitat variables, 5 traits (1 ordered, 4 factors)

• ade4::aviurba
51 sites, 11 environmental variables, 4 biological traits (factors)

• cluster::plantTraits
136 plant species, 31 biological attributes (morphological or reproductive)

• mvabund::antTraits	
Abundances of 41 epigaeic ant species across 30 sites in south-eastern Australia;
5 species trait; 7 environmental variables

• FD::dummy
Dummy Functional Trait Dataset; artificial data
8 species and 8 functional traits (2 continuous, 2 nominal, 2 ordinal, and 2 binary)

• FD::tussock
Functional Composition of Short-Tussock Grasslands
53 plant species in 30 plots, 16 functional traits (quantitative and factors)
